What is SOEN?

SOEN is an abbreviation of Software Engineering, its a degree course offered in some universities in kenya like, JKUAT, DEDAN KIMATHI, MOI university ...Its a four year course aimed at equipping a student wiht adequate knowledge and skills on how to write programs that can solve real life problems.

Why learn here?
